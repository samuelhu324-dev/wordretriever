The Company

Our client is a tech focused business searching for talented engineers across the market. With strong foundations already in place, they're now searching for a software engineer to join their team who are responsible for integration, ingestion and validation of data across the various internal systems. If you love to tailor solutions, innovate and work on AI, this could be the perfect role for you.


The Role

In this dynamic role, you'll face new challenges every day, applying a strong process-driven mindset while continuously looking for smarter, more innovative ways to improve how things are done. As part of the global team, you'll play a key role in driving innovation - experimenting with and operationalizing AI alongside ensuring the data platform is ready for business stakeholders to use. This is the perfect role for a hybrid engineer who has experience in software engineering alongside creating data pipelines and ingestions.

Your Skills & Experience

4+ years of experience as a software engineer working in a data heavy business, ideally in high-volume or distributed systems environments
Strong programming skills in Python or Java
Experience with creating ETL pipelines, extracting, ingesting, transforming and ensuring it's ready to be used by downstream stakeholders
Experience with Kafka for event streaming
A collaborative mindset-comfortable working across domains, products, and infrastructure layers.

About You

You have exceptional technical know-how and a real passion for the end-to-end solutions that drive the user experience and customer happiness. You will have effective written and oral communication skills for use in a complex project environment.
