Work with major Australian brands on innovative, market‑leading products in a high‑ambition, no‑nonsense and supportive culture, where your ideas are valued and your growth is actively supported.

WHAT WE DO

At Holocentric, we develop products that give organizations an asymmetric advantage by enabling faster, cheaper, and more robust operational, risk, and compliance decisions at scale. We are proud to have long-term partnerships with many iconic Australian brands across banking, airlines, utilities, universities, and government sectors. Backed by Constellation Software, a leading listed software company, we anticipate accelerated growth with new products and business models in the coming years.



WHAT IS IT LIKE WORKING IN OUR TEAM?

Join a motivated, practical team with bold yet realistic goals. We openly discuss priorities and focus on what matters most.

Collaboration is key: flat hierarchy, no politics, and every voice counts. Support, feedback, and coaching are standard. You’re never alone when challenges arise.

We celebrate all wins and embrace learning from mistakes. We prefer trying and failing over analysis paralysis to drive innovation.

We value doing right by colleagues and customers, with transparency and a no-nonsense attitude. We work hard to keep our promises.



WHAT YOU WILL BE WORKING ON

In the immediate future, you will be working as part of a new team consisting of driven analysts, designers, engineers, and testers. This team will work on a high-growth venture focused on helping organizations make critical, high-stakes decisions at a speed enabled by artificial intelligence. The venture will incorporate various elements including decision intelligence, decision governance, data fabric, and artificial intelligence technologies. This team will develop products using Lean Startup principles and Agile methodologies to ensure rapid iteration and responsiveness to customer needs.



ABOUT THE ROLE

As a DevOps Engineer, you will help implement and maintain DevSecOps practices within the R&D team. This role focuses on implementing technical solutions for cloud platforms and infrastructure automation, supporting internal and external stakeholders while assisting with Cyber Security and Compliance initiatives across the organization. 



WHAT YOU WILL DO

Platform Engineering & Cloud Solutions 

Implement cloud solutions that align with business needs, best practices, and industry standards 

Assist in reducing costs and risks while maintaining performance and reliability of resources 

Security & Compliance Support 

Assist in implementing and managing security measures across products and infrastructure 

Support penetration testing, code and vulnerability scanning activities across products 

Collaborate with the compliance team to meet contractual, regulatory, and industry standard frameworks  

Infrastructure & Systems Implementation 

Implement logging, monitoring, and alerting systems to maintain the health of application environments and infrastructure 

Provide first-line support and troubleshooting for applications and infrastructure 

Technical Implementation 

Utilize AI tools for vulnerability assessments, infrastructure automation, and deployment pipelines

Apply understanding of networking concepts and cloud security practices 

Support adoption of DevSecOps practices across the organization 



WHAT YOU WILL NEED FOR SUCCESS

To succeed, you’ll need

a passion for products, customer delivery and success

a passion for solving business problems with technology

a bachelor’s degree or equivalent education or training in Information Technology related fields (e.g. computer science), or experience in software or front-end / website development.

Technical Competencies  

Strong foundations in cloud platforms including Azure with advanced infrastructure management

Developing Infrastructure as Code (IaC) implementation skills using Terraform and similar tools

Working knowledge of observability and monitoring solutions including Datadog and similar platforms

Understanding of DevSecOps principles, security frameworks, and system design concepts

Required Qualifications / Experience

Relevant tertiary qualifications in Business and/or Technology (eg. Bachelor of Science in Computing or Information Systems)

Demonstrated experience as a DevOps Engineer

2-5 years of DevOps, Infrastructure, or related technical experience
